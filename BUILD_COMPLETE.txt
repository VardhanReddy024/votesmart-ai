HOW TO USE THE APP
================================================================================

1. Open index.html
2. Enter your age (number between 0-120)
3. Enter your state (text input)
4. Select "Yes" or "No" for first-time voter
5. Click "Get My Voting Guide"
6. Review your personalized voting steps
7. Check off documents as you gather them
8. Ask questions using the chat assistant
9. Click "Clear Form & Start Over" to reset
